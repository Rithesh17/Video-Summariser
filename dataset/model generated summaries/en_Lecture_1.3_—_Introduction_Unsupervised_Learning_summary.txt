- Unsupervised learning involves analyzing labeled data without guidance on what to do.
- Clustering is an unsupervised learning technique that groups data points into distinct clusters.
- Google News uses clustering to categorize news stories based on content.
- Unsupervised learning aids in understanding genomics by identifying patterns in gene expression data.
- Social network analysis and market segmentation utilize unsupervised learning to identify groups and segments within data.
- The cocktail party algorithm separates audio sources from multiple recordings without prior knowledge of the individual voices.
- Octave, a free and open-source programming environment, simplifies the implementation of machine learning algorithms.
